Neon Drift Pulse

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

Neon Drift Pulse is a single-player arcade game. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app uses, when information may be shared with service providers, and what choices you have.

Information Stored On Device

The game stores gameplay information such as your high score, selected theme, and revive state locally on your device so the app can remember your progress between sessions.

Advertising

The app uses rewarded ads for optional revives. Watching an ad is optional. When ads are enabled, an ad provider such as Google Mobile Ads may collect limited device identifiers, diagnostics, ad performance events, and fraud-prevention signals according to that provider’s own policies.

Tracking And Permissions

If the app requests App Tracking Transparency permission, it will do so before using tracking-enabled advertising features. You can deny that permission and continue using the game.

How Information Is Used

Information is used to operate the game, save progress, load rewarded ads, measure ad performance, prevent abuse, and improve app stability.

Data Sharing

Gameplay progress stored locally is not sold. If you watch a rewarded ad, limited technical and advertising-related information may be shared with the ad provider to deliver and measure that ad.

Children

The game is not intended to collect personal information directly from children. If you believe information was provided in error, contact support and request deletion guidance.

Your Choices

You can stop using rewarded ads by not tapping the revive option, reset local game data by removing the app, and manage ad or tracking permissions through iOS Settings.

Contact

For privacy questions, support issues, or ad complaints, contact peso-41-tortes@icloud.com.
